Video & Music Downloader

What you can do
- Download videos in: 720p, 1080p, 1440p, or 4K
- Download music as MP3 in: 128, 192, 256, or 320 kbps
- Download single videos OR whole playlists
- Cancel a playlist download if you started the wrong one

How it works (simple)
- Copy a video/playlist link
- Click the app icon in your system tray (near the clock)
- Choose Video / Audio (or Playlist) and pick the quality

Where files go
- Saves downloads to your Windows “Downloads” folder by default

Extra
- Optional “Autostart” toggle so it can start with Windows
- Advanced users can run it with command-line options too
